Frequently
asked questions

All your questions are already answered, check them out!

What's the price of enifty card?

The price of one Alpha Coin is dependent upon the supply and demand of the coins. It can vary, just like other utility tokens in the market.

Can more enifty cards be generated?

No, there is no possibility of creating more Alpha Coins, this is encoded in the contract.

Are enifty cards a good investment?

The success of the project depends on many factors. We do not have a crystal ball so it is impossible to know how it will go, but we strongly believe in our project and think it has a bright future ahead, but ultimately you will have to decide for yourself.

Can I become an affiliate partner?

Yes you can! We’ve got a great affiliate deal to benefit you and your friends that are eager to join. Open your dashboard and check the affiliate section on how to get started.

On which chain is the ecosystem built?

Ethereum. Most people use Ethereum, so it’s most easy to use. The contract will be optimized to minimize gas fees. There are other chains that have lesser gas fees, but ease of use is more important to us.
